Our mission
Develop Digital solution for Healthcare

We apply digital technologies, including mobile apps, wearable devices, and sensors, to improve healthcare delivery and patient outcomes.

Our filed encompasses a wide range of applications, including telemedicine, remote monitoring, digital therapeutics, and personalized medicine. Digital medicine has the potential to revolutionize healthcare by enabling more precise and personalized diagnosis and treatment, improving patient engagement and adherence, and reducing healthcare costs.

In Oxford, UK 2022, with the support of Professor Andrew Farmery, our group was initiated to develop novel digital applications for Anaesthesia and Neuroscience applications.
